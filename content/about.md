## Who am I?

{{< bio img="images/me.png" >}}

I'm David Pérez, an engineer living in the beautiful city of Barcelona, Spain. I hold a BSc in Aerospace Engineering and a MSc in Automatic Control, both from the [Polytechnic University of Catalonia](https://www.upc.edu/en). I have some experience in Computer Vision (both Deep Learning and traditional), but most of my career has been revolving the different technical layers that compose modern Satellite Navigation Receivers, aka [GNSS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Satellite_navigation). 

That includes embedded software running on real-time systems (FreeRTOS in my case); integrating third-party sensors, like commercial GNSS receivers and inertial sensors; sensor fusion; and raw satellite RF measurement and data extraction.


If you like what you see and think that we could work together, **feel free to contact me**. I'm open to consulting or contractor roles.
